‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How many companions can I have?

A: 1-4 companions (default: 2). Configure via SVM mod settings. Requires BigBrain, Waypoints, and SVM dependencies.

Q: What is the Evac Sequence?

A: When critically wounded (under 20% health), your squad has a chance to rescue you. Experience black phases, combat voice lines, heartbeat audio, and tunnel vision as they drag you to extract. Opt-in feature.

Q: How do gesture commands work?

A: Use F1 gesture wheel. Loot/CheckHim puts squad in Holding with Worker role looting. HoldPosition creates security perimeter. FollowMe/Regroup returns to Follow mode. Suppress forces Active combat.

Q: Is healing really free?

A: Yes. Your squad medic has dedicated supplies (concept). Use side-heal (automatic within 3m when you heal) or manual heal (look at companion, press H). Prevents negative medkit bugs.

Q: Do companions loot automatically?

A: Yes (default ON). In AO Mode, only Worker role loots during Holding phase. Simple Mode allows all companions to loot when safe. They gear themselves up from battlefields.

Q: What's the Area of Operations system?

A: AO system has 5 states: Active (combat), Clearing (searching), Holding (post-combat roles), Retreating (withdraw), Dormant (transit). Enable with AdvancedCombatTactics setting.

Q: Will this work with Fika multiplayer?

A: Evac sequence needs network sync for multiplayer. Core companion features may work but are untested. Designed primarily for single-player SPT.
