‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How many companions can I have?

A: Currently up to 4 companions are fully active and working in raids. This can be configured server-side.

Q: Do companions actually spawn in raids?

A: Yes! Companions spawn with you and are fully functional AI PMCs. They fight alongside you, use cover, and coordinate with SAIN AI.

Q: How do I manage my squad?

A: Use the in-game chatbot system. Your companions appear in your friend list and can be managed via commands.

Q: What happens if my companion dies?

A: Death is permanent for that raid. They won't respawn until your next raid.

Q: Do companions extract with me?

A: Yes, companions that survive will extract with you at the extraction point.

Q: Will this work with multiplayer (Fika)?

A: Fika compatibility is planned and architecture is in place, currently in testing phase.
